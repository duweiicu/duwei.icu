DUWEI.ICU

Du Wei, the director of the International department of Qingdao Academy, was praised by all the students for his many "glorious deeds".

In order to let more people know Du Wei, we created this website. ICU means Intensive Care Unit or I See You, depending on you.

This website is created by students of the International department of Qingdao Academy, who are not affiliated with any individual or organization. The purpose of this website is to make Qingdao Academy International department a better place.

This website will also include some teachers who are loved by students of the International department. We only target a few teachers, and we hope that this website will not cause trouble to the teachers who are mentioned because they are praised by students, or the teachers who are not mentioned.

To contribute to this website, please submit some events to us by:

  • Send an email to doctor@duwei.icu (Due to the network environment - Great Firewall of China, it is recommended to use Outlook, Gmail, and other overseas email services to ensure delivery)
  • Open a Pull Request on GitHub

To prevent you from being bothered by the submission to the website, it is recommended to use the third person to describe the event and omit the content that may contain personal information; we guarantee that we will not disclose any personal information of the contributor, unless the contributor actively requests to be identified (in fact, the above two ways of submission can be guaranteed to be anonymous through their own operation).
